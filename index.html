Our mission

English education is a privilege in Indonesia. We are fixing that.

Founded by Megan Griffith — 8 years international English teaching, previously published in Japan. Auravale Academy makes CEFR-aligned, neuroscience-informed books affordable and accessible, starting in Indonesia and expanding across Southeast Asia.
